Warning!

A staff illness policy or procedure helps you to:

The food business must tell all staff about their duties for:

  • good personal hygiene
  • illness
  • hand washing and
  • notifying their supervisor if they have, or think they have, contaminated food.

If the staff are from non-English-speaking backgrounds, the information should be provided in the relevant languages (if possible).

The free online training DoFoodSafely can help staff understand their duties and responsibilities. It is also available in other languages.

We recommend you keep a record of training provided. Template logs are available in the resources list to assist you.
